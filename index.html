My dearest Aarati,
There are moments that stay with you not because they were loud, but because they were yours. The quiet ones — the walks, the silences between words, the way you look at the world and somehow make it softer — those are the moments I keep returning to. I did not plan to fall for you. But you crept in slowly, and now I find pieces of you in every good thing. In the warmth of a song that hits just right. In the glow of a morning that feels borrowed from somewhere beautiful. Loving you has been the easiest thing I have ever done. And I would choose it, again and again, without hesitation. You are home. You are my favourite story. You are every reason I believe that something as simple as two people finding each other can be the grandest thing in the world. Always yours,
Aaryan 🌸
